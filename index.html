Your Questions, Answered

Which structure is best for tax optimization?

Dubai offers freezone companies (100% foreign ownership, 0% corporate tax on qualifying income) and mainland companies (broader market access). For pure tax optimization, freezone structures like IFZA or DMCC are ideal.

What's the approximate cost of setup?
  • Freezone packages: AED 12,000 - 35,000/year
  • Mainland setups: AED 25,000 - 60,000
What documents are required for company incorporation?

Standard requirements: Passport copies, proof of address (utility bill/bank statement), business plan, and company name reservation.

We provide a personalized checklist once you book a strategy call.

How long does the entire process take?

14 days on average for freezone companies. Mainland setups take 21-28 days. Corporate banking adds 7-10 days post-license issuance.

Do I need to be physically present in Dubai?

Not necessarily. Many freezones allow 100% remote incorporation. However, you will need to visit for Emirates ID registration and potentially bank account opening (typically 1 visit for 3-5 days).
